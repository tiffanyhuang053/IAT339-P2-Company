Matcha Tiramisu

CAD $8.00

Light, layered, and amazingly delicious! Our matcha tiramisu is a fun twist on the classic Italian dessert. We soak our Savoiardi biscuits in a smooth matcha and layer it with home whipped mascarpone cream. The top is generously dusted with our ceremonial grade Japanese matcha, giving each bite a balance between sweet goodness from the cream and umami from the matcha infusions.
