The Matcha Lab

Who we are.

As a person, the company is calm, easygoing, and approachable. It values taking things one step at a time, living in the moment, and bringing creativity into each and every product and experience it offers.

What we do.

The Matcha Lab is a specialty cafe with a wide range of handcrafted matcha products. From baked goods to drinks, the Matcha Lab prides itself in using ceremonial grade matcha from our very own matcha farm in Uji, Kyoto. We strive to create a calm, comforting environment for our customers.

Why you choose us.

Our warm, calm space, mixed with thoughtfully crafted, high quality products, fosters a welcoming environment inviting customers to slow down and enjoy the moment.
