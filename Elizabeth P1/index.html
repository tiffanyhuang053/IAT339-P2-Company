Branding

The Company

What does the company do, or offer?

MEBO is a pet memorial company focusing on creating cloud-shaped ceramic urns and candle holders. Pet owners can keep pet hair, whiskers, or ashes in the urn. The package also includes dried flowers symbolizing eternal love, a memorial card as a handmade memorial plaque for the pet, and a seed card to let the pet accompany their owner in another form.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Targeted towards young pet owners, MEBO aims to act as a friend by the side of owners who have just lost their pet. Offering emotional support through honoring the memories owners have with their pet. Every time they see the memorial box, it provides a source of comfort knowing their pet is with them.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

MEBO is special in providing a unique cloud-shaped design representing the softness and tranquility of clouds. As well as personalization to inscribe each pet's name on the urn. The small pet figure sitting on top of the urn reminds owners of the companionship they shared with their pet.

Customer Persona

Christy lives alone in a small apartment in the busy center of Toronto. Working remotely as a software engineer, she spends most of her time at home and rarely goes outdoors. For the past nine years, her Yorkshire Terrier, Coco, has been her closest companion. When Coco passed away due to old age, Christy was overwhelmed with grief. The once-familiar apartment felt unbearably quiet, intensifying her sense of loss. Wanting a meaningful way to honor Coco, Christy followed a close friendâ€™s recommendation and discovered MEBO. Drawn to its gentle design and calming color, she chose a blue memorial urn. Now, as Christy works at her desk, the soft blue urn sits nearby. Each time she glances up from her screen, she is comforted by the feeling that Coco is still with her.
